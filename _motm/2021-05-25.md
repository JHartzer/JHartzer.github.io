A note from the editor:

Hello fellow memers. Today marks the 75th Meme of the Monday, an auspicious milestone. On this momentous day, I have bittersweet news from the Meme of the Monday staff. Due to macroenvironmental factors, this 75th meme will be our final meme. Meme of the Monday started as a nothing but a dream and from our humble beginnings in October of 2019, we've grown over the past year and a half to reach over 70 people. It has been a whirlwind serving this group. So it is with a heavy heart that we must say goodbye. I take comfort in knowing that so long as there is humor in the world, the spirit of Meme of the Monday will never truly die. Thank you for allowing me to serve you and going on this journey together. It has been an honor.
